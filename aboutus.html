About Us

At TNPL Travel, our mission is to make traveling your dream destinations simple, affordable, and stress-free. We understand that not everyone has the time or resources to plan a perfect trip months in advance. That’s why we offer ready-made travel packages as well as personalized trip planning services, allowing you to explore the world while paying in flexible installments. Whether you want to follow one of our popular packages or plan a completely custom trip, our team is here to help you turn your travel dreams into reality.

How It Works

  1. Choose from our ready-made packages or request a custom trip.
  2. Pay a planning fee if using a custom trip service.
  3. Confirm your itinerary and travel arrangements.
  4. Pay remaining installments while enjoying your trip.

Our Packages

At TNPL Travel, we offer three ways to enjoy your dream trip. Start Your Journey lets you plan your trip yourself using our ready-made packages, giving you full control over your adventure. Plan With Us provides a personalized experience, where our team creates a custom itinerary tailored to your preferences and schedule. Finally, our Packages are popular, pre-planned trips designed for convenience and stress-free travel. No matter which option you choose, we make it simple and affordable to explore your dream destinations.

Planning Fee

For a fully personalized experience, TNPL Travel offers a custom trip planning service. Our team will create a complete itinerary tailored to each traveler’s dream destination, activities, and schedule. This service comes with a $25 fee per person, ensuring that each traveler receives expert guidance and a stress-free travel plan.

Payment Options

We accept secure payments through credit and debit cards. Additional digital wallets like Apple Pay or Google Pay may also be available. All transactions are processed safely via Stripe, ensuring your information is protected.

Refund Policy

At TNPL Travel, we strive to make your experience stress-free. Please note the following regarding refunds:

  • The $25 custom trip planning fee is non-refundable, unless we fail to provide a complete planning service or travel plan as promised.
  • Refunds for travel packages depend on the provider’s terms and may vary. Please review your package details carefully before booking.
  • If you need to cancel or change your trip, please contact us via email one week before your scheduled departure. We will assist you based on the package or service rules.

Contact Us

Email us at tnpltravel@gmail.com for questions or custom trip requests.

FAQ

  • Can I cancel anytime? Check package terms for details.
  • Are deposits refundable? Deposits are refundable only according to package rules.
  • When do I pay installments? Payment schedules depend on the package or custom plan agreed upon.
