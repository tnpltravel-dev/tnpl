About Us

TNPL Travel was created to help people dream big and travel stress-free. Many people cannot travel due to time, money, or planning difficulties. We provide flexible travel solutions and payment installments so everyone can explore their dream destinations.

How It Works

  1. Choose from our ready-made packages or request a custom trip.
  2. Pay a deposit or planning fee if using a custom trip service.
  3. Confirm your itinerary and travel arrangements.
  4. Pay remaining installments while enjoying your trip.

Our Packages

Choose from our popular, ready-made travel packages to hit your dream destinations, or plan a fully custom trip with us.

Planning Fee

Custom trip planning incurs a fee of $25 per person. This fee covers personalized itinerary creation, hotel & activity suggestions, and consultation.

Payment Options

We accept credit/debit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. For installment plans, you can pay in small amounts while traveling.

Refund Policy

The planning fee is non-refundable. Deposits and packages may have specific refund rules — please check details for each package.

Contact Us

Email us at chamakalon@gmail.com for questions or custom trip requests.

FAQ

  • Can I cancel anytime? Check package terms for details.
  • Are deposits refundable? Deposits are refundable only according to package rules.
  • When do I pay installments? Payment schedules depend on the package or custom plan agreed upon.
